Creative Enclosure

For my creative enclosure, I wanted to make an effect where the LED would light up a skyscraper. So I sketched out the skyline and then boxed in the breadboard and Arduino. I then poked a hole through the top to put in a pencil to activate the different effects of the LED. The outcome was a box with a light that lighted up the tallest skyscraper drawn.
