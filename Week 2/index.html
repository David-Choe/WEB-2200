Settlers of Catan

The Settlers of Catan or Catan is a popular board game designed by Klaus Teuber. In this multiplayer game the player are settlers, attempting to build and develop their settlements. This is done with gathering resources and trading. Points are given with settlements, upgraded settlements, longest roads, largest armys, and development cards. The first to 10 points win in the base game.

Gameplay

At the start of the game the player sets up the board. The board is made up of hexigonal pieces allowing the board to be randomize each playthrough. Once the board is made players go in order playing down their first settlements, these settlement will determine the resources that the player will get. Each hexigonal tile of the board will have number assoicated with it. When the dice is rolled the number will determine which resources a player will get. Player settlements placement is important as it will determine what resources will be avaible for the player. Seven is the only number in the game in which player will not get resources as it will allow the player to move a robber that can block certain tiles from giving resources. Players will continue to roll and gather resources and build settlements and road in order to win the game.
